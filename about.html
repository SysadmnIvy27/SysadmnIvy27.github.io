About me

Hi, my name is Augustus, and I'm a junior at Lawrenceburg High School and a student of Ivy Tech Community College in Lawrenceburg Indiana. This site has been a project of mine for the past four months, and it aims to provide a suitable place for my family to access sites and plan things out together.

FAQ

Q: Why this site?

A: I wanted to create something personalized and tailored to our family unlike Nextcloud, and sometimes the easiest way to make something you like is to do it yourself.

Q: Will more features be added in the future?

A: No, the site is meant to be barebones and to the point. Small tweaks may be added, but otherwise no major features or new pages will be added unless absolutly necessary.

Q: How do I find the family events?

A: Look under the family calender found here.
